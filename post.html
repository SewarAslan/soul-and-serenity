Breathe Easy: Simple Breathing Techniques to Reduce Stress

In our busy lives, stress can sneak up on us, often when we least expect it. One of the simplest, most effective ways to counteract stress is through mindful breathing techniques. Here’s how you can incorporate these quick, calming methods into your day.

How we breathe affects us at a cellular level. Research shows changing the way we breathe can influence weight, athletic performance, allergies, asthma, snoring, mood, stress, focus and so much more. You can learn to breathe better and these exercises can help.

Source: The New York Times

Box Breathing

Box breathing, also known as four-square breathing, is a technique that helps you focus and calm your mind. Here’s how to practice:

How to Practice:

  • Inhale slowly for 4 counts.
  • Hold the breath for 4 counts.
  • Exhale slowly for 4 counts.
  • Hold again for 4 counts.
