Box Breathing

Box breathing, also known as four-square breathing, is a technique that helps you focus and calm your mind.

How to Practice:

  • Inhale slowly for 4 counts.
  • Hold the breath for 4 counts.
  • Exhale slowly for 4 counts.
  • Hold again for 4 counts.
